Why Specialty Items


We sell 2nd hand flooring. 2nd hand material is sold cheaply and is as good as the 1st hand products sold by the big box stories.

What are 2nd hand products


The carpet industry considers a perfect roll of carpet 1st hand quality. If that roll has a minor defect like discoloration or a mark then it is considered 2nd hand quality.

We take the 2nd hand material, find the defect, and remove it. This takes a lot of work so the major carpet industry has decided that it's just cheaper for them to sell it as a 2nd hand product

This is why we are able to sell top quality material at cheaper prices
